The mathematical formula for compound interest with monthly contributions is:

$$A = P(1 + \frac{r}{n})^{nt} + PMT \times \frac{(1 + \frac{r}{n})^{nt} - 1}{\frac{r}{n}}$$

Where $A$ is the final amount, $P$ is the principal investment, $r$ is the annual interest rate, $n$ is the number of times interest is compounded per year, and $t$ is the number of years.
