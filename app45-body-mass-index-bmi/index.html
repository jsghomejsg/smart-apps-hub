What is Body Mass Index (BMI)?

BMI is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. Our 2026 BMI Calculator helps you determine if you are in a healthy weight range. The formula is universal: weight in kilograms divided by the square of height in meters.

How to interpret your results?

According to health standards, a BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered normal. Below 18.5 is underweight, and over 25 is considered overweight. Use this BMI tool to track your fitness goals this year.
