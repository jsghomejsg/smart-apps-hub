How to calculate your due date in 2026?

Planning for a new family member is an exciting journey. Our 2026 Pregnancy Calculator uses the standard 280-day rule from your last menstrual period (LMP) to estimate your delivery date. While only a small percentage of babies are born on their exact due date, having an estimated baby arrival date helps you prepare your home and schedule for the big day.
