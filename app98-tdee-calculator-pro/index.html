Understanding Energy Balance in 2026

Your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is an estimation of how many calories you burn per day when exercise is taken into account. Our 2026 Fitness Tool uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, widely considered the most accurate for Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) calculation.

$$BMR = 10 \times weight + 6.25 \times height - 5 \times age + s$$

Where $s$ is +5 for males and -161 for females. By knowing your maintenance calories, you can scientifically approach your weight loss or muscle gain goals. Tracking your macros and caloric intake is the foundation of modern nutrition and professional body composition management in 2026.
