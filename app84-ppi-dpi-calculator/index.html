Why does PPI matter for your screen in 2026?

The Pixels Per Inch (PPI) or Dots Per Inch (DPI) determines how sharp a screen looks to the human eye. Our 2026 PPI Tool uses the Pythagorean theorem to find the diagonal resolution and divides it by the screen size. Higher PPI means more detail and smoother text. Whether you are choosing between a 4K monitor or a Retina display smartphone, knowing the pixel density helps you understand the true visual quality of the hardware.
