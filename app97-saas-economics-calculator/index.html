The Science of Scalable Growth in 2026

In the high-stakes world of SaaS and subscription businesses, understanding your unit economics is the difference between failure and a billion-dollar exit. Our 2026 SaaS Metrics Tool provides an instant analysis of your Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).

A healthy business typically aims for an LTV/CAC ratio of 3:1 or higher. If your ratio is lower, you might be spending too much to acquire low-value customers. Our tool also factors in Churn Rate, the silent killer of growth, showing you how long it takes to recover your marketing spend (Payback Period).
